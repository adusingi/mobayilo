Immediate benefits for the team

Easy Maps

Easy and intelligent maps visualization. Optimize all your intra and inter-frequency neighbour relations easily. Display all Network parameters, KPIs, parameters changes on your maps.

Parameters

The application can parse all type of dumps files. Parameters are easy to check thanks to our powerful search function. Compare and Audit your network configuration in a very fast and simple way.

Dashboard

View the overall network KPI data, Accessibility, Retainability, Mobility, Traffic, Throughput... in one place with the customizable dashboard. Create template for following up your daily activities.

Communication

All the necessary information in one place and shared between the team. Clear Follow up activities and to-do list management for each member.


Powerful Customizable Dashboard

Powerful dashbord to understand in a glance the state and Network performance. Customizable dasboard for your daily follow-up activities, integration, Worst cell activities,.... Share your dashboard with the team and the customer to Report the Network performance.

Better Interaction with Maps

Optimize all your intra and inter-frequency neighbords relations on your maps easily. Create your own layer by simple query and customize the view depending your daily activities

Real-time Communication with your team

Share documents, discuss ongoing activities and future projects, see what's up with your team. Plan and share your to do list activites for management to forecast workload.
