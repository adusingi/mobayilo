We believe that Engineers should have great tools, ease to use to make work more pleasant

Mobayilo is a web application that provides all the tools for Mobile Network Optimization, Integration and Maintenance teams to get things done. The application provide all network information in one place and synchronize teams. Our purpose is to make telecom engineers work environment more productive, collaborative, pleasent and simpler.

  • Simple to use and intuitive interface
  • Collaborative and pleasant
  • All information up-to-date and synchronize between team.

Immediate benefits for your team

Easy Maps

Easy and intelligence maps visualization. Optimize all your intra and inter-frequency neighbour relations easily. Display all Network parameters, KPIs, parametres changes on your maps.

Parameters

The application can parse all the Configuration Management file (CM) from all vendors. Parameters are easy to access and check thanks to our powerful search function and interface. Compare and Audit your network configuration very fast and simple. Parameter activities update and synchronise within the team. .

Dashboard

View the overall network KPI data, Accessibility, Retainability, Mobility, Traffic, Throughput... in one place with the customizable dashboard. Create template for following up your daily activities.

Communication

All the necessary information in one place and shared between the team. Follow up activites and to do list management clear for each member.

Powerful Customizable Dashboard

Powerful dashbord to understand in a glance the states and Network performance. Customize dasbord for your daily follow-up activities, integration, Worst cell activities,.... Share your dashbaord with team and the customer to Report the Network performance.

Better Interaction with Maps

Optimize all your intra and inter-frequency neighbords relations on your maps easily. Create your own layer by simple query and customize the view depending your daily activities

Real-time Communication with your team

Share documents, discuss ongoing activities and future projects, see what's up with your team. Plan and share your to do list activites for management to forecast workload..
